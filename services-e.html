Hole-punching

We can punch customized holes in your documents for binders and folders of various shapes and sizes. Please provide us with a binder or folder as an example.

Scoring

Scoring is the process of making a groove in documents where the paper must be folded in order to get a clean fold and ensure that the paper does not rip. The paper can then be folded with a folding machine or by hand.

Perforation

If your document contains a section to be detached, we can perforate that section to make it easier to separate.

Lamination

We can laminate documents up to 24 inches wide.

Shredding

If you have confidential documents that need to be shredded, please contact the Material Management and Logistics Office by email: asstmgt@sen.parl.gc.ca

To place an order for paper, ink or other supplies (e.g. staples), please contact the Client Service Centre. When ordering supplies, consult the information sheet posted next to the photocopier and include the ID number assigned to it (click on the image below to view in detail).

Mailing lists

Do you have a householder you would like to send out? We can help! We can print addresses from your database on letters and envelopes.

You must provide Printing Services with your mailing list (.txt file). Information Services can explain to you how to prepare your database, as needed.

The following envelope sizes are available:

  • 5.5” x 8.5”

  • 9” x 12”
  • 9.5” x 15”

Scanning

Do you need to scan a photo for one of your documents or include an article in a press kit? We can scan your documents and save them in PDF, JPEG and TIFF formats. Our professional equipment produces high-resolution images. The scanned files will be emailed to you or downloaded directly onto your computer by Information Services.

Photos and signatures can be scanned in colour, and reference documents and articles can be scanned in black and white.

Here are some examples of things that can be scanned:

  • Photos and drawings
  • Signatures
  • Old documents
  • Fragile documents
  • Legal materials for reports
  • Old printed files
  • Reference articles
  • Newspaper clippings
  • Certificates
  • And so much more

Given that this service can take a while, depending on the number of documents to be scanned, please provide us with some advance notice.

Note: Please ensure that all requests comply with the Copyright Act before sending them to us and publishing any scanned material.
