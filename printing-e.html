Committee reports

Committees form the cornerstone of the Senate’s work. They are recognized for their major contribution to legislation, public policy and political issues.

We print committee reports in various formats.

Please contact us for more information on how copies are distributed. You can also download or view all committee reports on our website: sen.parl.gc.ca.

Committee reports can be bound one of three ways (see this page):

  • Thermo-binding
  • Spiral binding
  • Saddle stitch

Newsletters

Senators’ newsletters are an effective communication tool for keeping the people of your district informed of your activities in the Senate.

  • Newsletters can be printed in various formats, in colour or black and white.
  • The contents must be related to Senate activities and must not contain any partisan material.

Large format printing

Our large format printer can print items up to 40 inches wide, depending on the desired length. Your large format documents can be printed in colour or black and white. These documents can also be glued to a stiff material called “foamcore”. Please contact Printing Services if you wish to submit any orders of this nature.

Christmas cards

Every year, we offer a variety of Christmas card styles for exclusive use by senators. The styles are emailed out in the fall, along with an order form. You can also get your own personalized cards.*

Each senator’s office is responsible for drafting the message to be printed in the cards. This message can be printed in the colour of your choice.

You can choose either blank or franked envelopes.

Each senator can print up to 2,000 cards per fiscal year.

Please contact Printing Services for more information.

* You must refer to the Copyright Act for all images used on your Christmas cards. All products must comply with the Document Printing and Duplicating Policy.
