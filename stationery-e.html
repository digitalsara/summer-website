Personalized stationery

A variety of personalized products

Personalized stationery can include many items you use in your office every day: business cards, letterhead, envelopes, compliments cards, notebooks, thank you cards, and so much more!

This section presents the templates available, divided into two categories:

  • Templates for senators
  • Templates for Administration staff

Products are available in various types of paper and paperboard. Please refer to the icons below that appear next to each product. You can also supply your own paper for printing.

  1. White paper or cover paper
  2. Ivory paper with 50% cotton fibre content
  3. Beige paper with 25% cotton fibre content
  4. Glossy cover paper

The following templates use the Senate Emblem. Please note that senators have the option to use the Coat of Arms of Canada.
